Pro Series

Master the Squat

Technical Execution

3 Step Sequence
01

The Setup

Position the barbell across your upper traps. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out. Brace your core as if preparing for impact.

02

The Descent

Hinge at the hips and bend knees simultaneously. Keep chest proud and back neutral. Lower until thighs are parallel to the floor or slightly below.

03

The Drive

Drive through the mid-foot, extending hips and knees simultaneously. Exhale at the top and maintain core tension throughout the ascent.

warning Critical Alerts

dangerous

Knees Caving In

Avoid 'valgus' collapse. Actively push knees outward to track over your toes to prevent ACL strain.

dangerous

Rounding the Back

Loss of spinal neutrality places extreme load on the lumbar discs. Keep your gaze forward and lats engaged.
